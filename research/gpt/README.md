# 🤖 GPT Independent Research (research/gpt/)

This directory belongs exclusively to investigations and experiments conducted by **GPT** models.

## Objectives
- Conduct independent research on FKT concepts.
- Document and prove mathematical foundations from a GPT perspective.
- Avoid modifying or interfering with investigations from other models (Claude, Gemini, DeepSeek).
